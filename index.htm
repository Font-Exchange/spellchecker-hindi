 Help Desk: 
Treat : Treatment extra space and Paragraph lines to find appropriate word from database.

• Option Box

First Word: Press control button while clicking the word if right side characters are correct. Left suggestions will be offered.
 Last Word: Press shift button while clicking the word if left side characters are correct. Right suggestions will be offered.
